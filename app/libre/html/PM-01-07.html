HALLUCINATION

The Shadow across the hedge
is just Light dressed in black.

To stop feeling lonely,
break a mirror.

Bells toll
when her clitoris is clutched.

Simplicity becomes clothed
with the flower of silence.

Use the magic needle
to embroider the eye on your tears.

You hammer your fingers into the music
and swallows appear.

Life has its own back pain:
Heal yourself with guitars,
with teenagers' blades
or with the echo of a saint.

In the mirror
you view your face from the other side.

If death is a thread,
you weave your own hieroglyph.

The devil spewing sparks
is a brilliant idea.

The illusion is a cobweb
which never ends.

Sorrows become foam
in the oceans.

To stop crying each sunrise,
silence turns into a rooster.

Gaze at the living: They are dead.
Gaze at the dead: They are living.

You search for darkness;
hard to touch yet
easy to feel.

You cure the crazy ones
with eau de silence
and they all
want the remedy.

You create, paint, and draw…

But in the end, you know well
that hope is a
kind whore.
